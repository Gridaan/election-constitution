Preamble

We, the administration and faculty of [School Name], in our collective commitment to fostering a vibrant, democratic, and value-based learning environment, hereby establish this Constitution. This document serves as the supreme law governing the selection, tenure, duties, and responsibilities of the Head Boy and Head Girl. Our objective is to empower students to lead with integrity, serve with humility, and act as ambassadors of our school’s values.

Key Definitions

The Role & Eligibility

Understanding the significance of the office and the qualifications required to hold it.

Nature of the Office

The Office of the Head Boy and Head Girl is a position of honor, trust, and immense responsibility. It is not merely a title but a call to service. The individuals who hold this office are expected to be the most responsible, trustworthy, and hardworking students in the school.

Role in Governance

The Head Boy and Head Girl play a crucial role in the school's governance structure. They are an integral part of the school’s leadership team, providing a formal platform for student input and feedback and acting as a bridge to the administration.

Eligibility & Qualifications

The Hybrid Selection Process

A merit-based process combining peer voting with rigorous evaluation to ensure our leaders are not only popular but also capable and prepared for the role.

Duties & Responsibilities

A multifaceted role covering ambassadorial, disciplinary, mentorship, and event management duties.

Distribution of Responsibilities

Accountability & Governance

Upholding the integrity of the office through a clear code of conduct and fair disciplinary procedures.
